Why Logistic Regression was chosen

After evaluating 6 classification algorithms, Logistic Regression was selected as the primary production model. It achieves the highest accuracy (99.40%) and perfect AUC-ROC calibration (99.99%), meaning its confidence scores are highly reliable for assessing student burnout risk. Unlike ensemble methods, it is faster to train, simpler to maintain, and provides transparent probability estimates essential for mental health applications. The model uses L2 regularization and class weighting to handle the severe class imbalance (Low: 51 vs High: 4311 in training) while remaining interpretable.

🔍 How SHAP (Explainer) is Implemented

We implement LinearSHAP (Lundberg & Lee, NeurIPS 2017) from scratch using Logistic Regression coefficients — no external shap library required. This is the exact, closed-form solution for linear models, mathematically faithful to the model's own learned weights.

1
Compute Background Mean from Training Data

Calculate E[x] — the mean value of each feature across all training samples. This serves as the baseline for comparison (the model's expected prediction on average data).

2
Apply LinearSHAP Formula

For each input sample and predicted class c: φi = W[c, i] × (xi − E[xi]), where W[c, i] is the learned coefficient for feature i from the Logistic Regression model. Each feature's contribution is directly proportional to its learned weight times its deviation from the mean.

3
Interpret Feature Contributions

Result: φ vector where φᵢ > 0 means feature i pushed toward higher burnout risk, and φᵢ < 0 means it reduced risk. The sum of all φᵢ equals the model's deviation from its baseline prediction, guaranteeing SHAP additivity.

4
Group Into Domains

Features are grouped into Academic Stress, Behavioural Patterns, and Lifestyle & Health. Group SHAP = sum of |φᵢ| for all features in that domain.

❓ Why am I not getting Low Risk even with good sleep and exercise?

The model's 5 most important features are all psychological indicators — not physical ones. Good sleep and exercise matter, but they cannot override severely high psychological scores.

🔴 BLOCKS Low Risk
  • ⬆ Emotional Fatigue ≥ 6
  • ⬆ Anxiety Score ≥ 6
  • ⬇ Mood Stability ≤ 5
  • ⬇ Motivation Level ≤ 4
  • ⬆ Overthinking Score ≥ 6
✅ Required for Low Risk
  • Emotional Fatigue: 1–3
  • Anxiety Score: 1–3
  • Mood Stability: 8–10
  • Motivation Level: 8–10
  • Sleep Quality: 7–10

Example that caused confusion: A response with sleep=8 hrs, exercise=8 days but Emotional Fatigue=8, Mood=3, Motivation=4 → Predicted High Risk. This is correct — those psychological scores indicate burnout symptoms regardless of how much the person sleeps or exercises. The model learned this pattern from 10,000 training samples.

📧 Email Notification System

BurnoutGuard sends automated HTML emails via Gmail SMTP (using an App Password). All emails are sent in a background thread — they never delay the web response. Configure BURNOUT_MAIL_SENDER and BURNOUT_MAIL_PASSWORD environment variables to enable.

🎉
Welcome Email Instant

Sent immediately on registration. Contains: how the system works, 4-step overview, link to Week 1 survey, username reminder.

📋
Week 1 Reminder 24hrs after signup

If the user registered but hasn't submitted Week 1 yet, a reminder is sent 24 hours later. Background scheduler checks every 6 hours.

📅
Weekly Survey Reminder Every 7 days

If the user submitted Week N but hasn't started Week N+1 after 7 days, an automated reminder is sent. Includes which week is due, time to complete, and dashboard link.

🔮
Prediction Result Email Instant after submission

Sent immediately after each survey. Contains: risk level (visual), confidence scores (Low/Medium/High %), top 3 SHAP-contributing features with direction, and recommendation summary. Links to full analysis.

⚠️
Trend Alert When risk worsens

Sent if trend changes to Declining or if risk jumps to High. Shows previous vs current risk, trend label, and calls to action. Not sent for stable/improving trends (to avoid notification fatigue).

🎓
Completion Certificate Email After Week 4

Sent when all 4 weeks are completed. Shows full week-by-week risk journey, final trend label, and links to the history page for the complete analysis.
